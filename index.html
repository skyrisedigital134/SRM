About Our School

SRM International School

"Every child is like a seed which has immense potential to grow and flourish when nurtured with love, care, and the right guidance."

SRM International School is committed to providing quality education that prepares students for a rapidly changing world. Located in the serene town of Sitarganj, Uttarakhand, our institution combines academic excellence with holistic development.

We believe that children must not only excel academically but also cultivate qualities such as courtesy, discipline, confidence, social responsibility, and a deep connection to the cultural values of our nation.

Our state-of-the-art facilities include modern classrooms, science and computer laboratories, a well-stocked library, and ample space for sports and extracurricular activities.

Our Story

A Journey Towards Excellence

"Every child is like a seed which has the potential to grow into a magnificent tree with the right care and nurturing."

SRM International School was established with a vision to provide quality education that is accessible, affordable, and focused on the all-round development of every student. Located in the serene town of Sitarganj in Uttarakhand, we have been shaping young minds with dedication and passion.

Our school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and offers classes from Nursery through Class XII. We combine modern pedagogical methods with time-tested values to create an environment where every child can thrive.

At SRM, we believe that education is not just about textbooks and examinations. It encompasses the development of character, creativity, critical thinking, and compassion. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow while keeping them rooted in the rich cultural traditions of our nation.
